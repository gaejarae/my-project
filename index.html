StockWars Multi-Agent AI Platform

Debate-based multi-agent investment decision platform deployed on AWS with Kubernetes. Designed to reduce single-model bias and provide transparent reasoning.

View Source

Project Overview

Traditional single-model investment AI systems often suffer from model bias and limited reasoning transparency. This project introduces a multi-agent debate architecture where specialized agents analyze finance, news, and charts independently before reaching a consensus.

Outcome

Implemented a debate-based decision system that improved reasoning transparency and demonstrated stronger edge-case handling compared to single-agent baseline models. Successfully deployed as a production-ready cloud service.
